Infomercials

MASS AUDIENCE = MASS SALES

If you want to sell your product on a mass scale with a potentially huge pay off, perhaps an infomercial is just what you need. Infomercials, also known as Direct Response TV, are television commercials which generally include a phone number or website.

There are long-form infomercials, which are typically between 15 and 30 minutes in length, and short-form infomercials, which are typically 30 seconds to 120 seconds in length. Creative Realm Entertainment makes the process easy to take your infomercial from script to screen.
